I obtained my Bachelors and Masters degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET).   • Motivations Behind My Research

    It's true that, processing power of a computer increased tremendously over the last few decades. Yet, it's far beyond the capability of a human brain, specially in visual scene understanding. A human brain can easily comprehend its surroundings and is capable of making quick decisions, which very hard and intractable in many cases even for the super fast computer. My research is driven by these facts and the following question: "How to build a machine with visual capability to the level of a human brain?"

  • My Achievements So Far

    My research interest lies in human activity recognition in continuous videos and object tracking in aerial videos. So far, I developed algorithms to detect and recognize human activities in surveillence videos, to track humans and vehicles in unstable aerial videos. Currently, I am working on incremental activity modeling in streaming videos.
